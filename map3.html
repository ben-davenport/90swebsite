Vegetative Carbon Sequestration

Map Methods

This map takes LandSat data and analyzes it using an automatic data clafficiation with bands [].

In essence, this uses light wavelengths to separates out different types of vegetation and concrete.

This map, and the accompanying analysis, gives a plausible estimate of the amount of vegetative carbon sequestration occuring within city boundaries in a calendar year.
