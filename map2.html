Green River Channel Migration

Map Methods

River channels are constantly changing across a range of space.

This study of the Green River in Washington is based on historic satellite imagery and maps.

Sinuosity is a measure of the amount of curvature, or 'channelization' of a river. More sinuosity is a sign that a rive is about to move to straighten. Darkest before the dawn and all that.
