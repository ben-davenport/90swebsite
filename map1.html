Potential Large Woody Debris in Plum Creek

Map Methods

This map takes LiDAR data from Oberlin, OH and maps the distance of large trees from a local creek. The distance from the creek, height of the trees, and number of trees of each height are mapped.

Mapping these trees helps show the potential distribution of large woody debris in Plum Creek. Large woody debris is essential to local ecology because it helps create small environments beneficial to beavers, birds, fish, and other local creatures.
